Our Methodology

Predictable execution, every engagement.

We follow Workday's proven six-phase delivery framework on every project. No surprises, no scope creep, just a repeatable structure your team can count on.

01

Plan

Requirements confirmed, RACI set, project plan in your hands by week one. Scope, assumptions, and responsibilities are agreed upfront.

02

Design

Future system owners review and approve the design before development begins.

03

Build

Built by senior Workday engineers and documented as we go.

04

Test

Unit, End to End, and UAT with documented test cases and defect logs. Business users participate throughout testing — they don't just sign off at the end.

05

Deploy

Cutover plan, rollback procedure, go/no-go checklist. We don't hand off until we're confident it'll hold in production.

06

Hypercare

Active monitoring and rapid response post-go-live. We stay engaged until the solution is performing as expected in production.
